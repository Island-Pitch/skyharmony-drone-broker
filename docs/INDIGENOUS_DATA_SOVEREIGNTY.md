# SkyHarmony — Indigenous Data Sovereignty Adoption Plan

## Te Mana Raraunga Alignment

SkyHarmony formally adopts the principles of **Te Mana Raraunga** (Maori Data Sovereignty Network) as our data governance framework.

> *"Our data, our sovereignty, our future."* — Te Mana Raraunga

### Principles Applied

| TMR Principle | SkyHarmony Implementation |
|--------------|--------------------------|
| Assert Maori rights in data | Operators own their data. RBAC ensures each operator controls access. |
| Safeguard and protect data | Zero outbound calls. Self-hosted. Encrypted at rest and in transit. |
| Ensure quality and integrity | Immutable audit trail on every change. |
| Advocate for governance | Uniform Terms. Transparent, versioned, auditable decisions. |
| Support data infrastructure | Cloud-agnostic Docker. No vendor lock-in. |
| Advance sustainable digital business | Transparent billing. Fair allocation. Cooperative economics. |

### CARE Principles

- **Collective Benefit** — Revenue split transparently across cooperative
- **Authority to Control** — Self-hosted, no cloud vendor access, data exportable
- **Responsibility** — Audit trail, maintenance triggers, incident accountability
- **Ethics** — Transparent billing, no hidden collection, privacy by design
